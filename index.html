PCBA Tester & Strain Analysis

  • FEM and LabVIEW/MATLAB pipeline before end-of-line release.

End Effector Design

Robotic end effector for handling high-voltage power modules inside a DC-DC charger and 1000 V DC charging stages.
  • Handles insulated power modules with defined orientation and preload.
  • Creepage and clearance respected in fork, clamp, and cable routing.
  • Mass optimised to stay inside the robot payload limits.
SolidWorks/NX GD&T High Voltage

02 / System Modelling

Matlab, Simulink & FEA

Matlab + Simulink Industrial Cooling

Dynamic model of a large-scale auxiliary cooling system with plate heat exchangers, pumps, and control valves for drivetrain testing.
  • Water–glycol mixture modelled in Simscape Fluids.
  • PID logic for pump speed and regulating valves.
  • Heat rejection capability of several megawatts.
Simulink Simscape Fluids PID Control

Matlab + Simulink Turbine Cooling

Thermal-hydraulic model of a wind turbine cooling circuit for converters, transformers, and auxiliary components.
  • Pressure-drop and flow distribution across branches.
  • Interaction with ambient wind speed and nacelle temperature.
  • Sizing support for pumps, hoses, and heat exchangers.
MATLAB Thermal Analysis Energy Systems

Bearing Inner-Ring Creep Simulation

FEA study of inner-ring creep in a wind-turbine main bearing using frictional contact and interference fits.
  • Ansys APDL scripting to automate load rotation.
  • Contact pressure, slippage, and hoop stress evaluation.
  • Comparison of different fit classes and surface conditions.
Ansys APDL FEA Contact Mechanics
